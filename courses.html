General Physics IV
(Vibration Waves and Optics)

This course provides a comprehensive pathway to learning the basics of vibration, waves and optics, covering topics such as types of waves, wave propagation, interference, diffraction and optical phenomena. Students will gain a deep understanding of the principles governing vibrations and waves, as well as the behavior of light and its applications in various fields.
